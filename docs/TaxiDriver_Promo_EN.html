Built for free roam

Not a scenario.
A complete mode.

TaxiDriver lives inside a dedicated in-game phone. Start and stop your shift whenever you want, on any compatible map with a usable road graph—without loading a separate scenario or relying on hard-coded coordinates.

01

Go online instantly

Add TaxiDriverHUD through UI Apps, press Start Driving, and let the dispatcher build a live pool of nearby work.

02

Choose your fare

Compare passenger, pickup time, distance, duration, payout, Calmness, stop count, and available bonuses before committing.

03

Drive with consequences

Your speed, impacts, braking, acceleration, and cornering affect the final fare, passenger satisfaction, and long-term rating.

A living dispatcher

Every request asks a different question.

Take the safe, predictable fare—or accept more pressure for more money. Request types are mixed into one gradually populated list instead of appearing as a repetitive scripted sequence.

Passengers, not markers

Every passenger brings a mood.

TaxiDriver adds personality without pretending to simulate a full human. Names, messages, Calmness, dissatisfaction, and reactions create enough variation to make each fare feel distinct.

Emoji chat

A message before you arrive

The selected passenger may send one to three coherent emoji-only messages while you drive to pickup. Each conversation keeps one mood and appears as a polished in-phone notification.

Ride quality matters

Earn the fare.
Keep the trust.

TaxiDriver is forgiving enough for city driving but rewards consistency. Short accidental speed spikes are filtered by tolerances and grace periods; real speeding, collisions, harsh maneuvers, and late pickups are recorded as clear penalty events.

The combined fare reduction is capped at 50%. A perfect 5.00 driver rating adds up to 15% to your earnings.

Navigation that adapts

A real map, built into the phone.

The stock BeamNG.drive minimap is adapted into a rectangular mobile layout and appears only when a route is active. It stays behind phone notifications and disappears cleanly on every non-navigation screen.

↕

Dynamic zoom

The map moves closer at low speed and smoothly zooms out as you accelerate, reaching its widest planned view at approximately 120 km/h.

ETA

Useful trip context

See arrival time, remaining distance, speed limit, route progress, and intermediate-stop markers calculated against the full ride.

OFF

Optional road arrows

Disable the navigation arrows drawn over the road when you want a cleaner driving view. The route remains visible on the phone map.

Designed to feel physical

Pickup is an event, not a checkpoint.

Arrive at the roadside point and stop. The phone changes state, the boarding loader begins, and TaxiDriver attempts to operate a suitable passenger door for a short arrival sequence.

Realistic roadside locations

Pickup, stop, and destination points prefer bus stops, parking spaces, and points of interest. Locations are projected toward the outer road edge instead of the middle of a multi-lane road.

  • No map-specific hard-coded coordinate list
  • Controlled variation along the same road segment
  • Recent locations are avoided when alternatives exist
  • Road-graph fallback keeps sparse maps playable

Boarding and drop-off

The app shows dedicated boarding and alighting screens while the selected passenger door opens and closes. Vehicles without a compatible trigger continue the ride normally.

  • Passenger pickup deadline and late-arrival penalty
  • Best-effort passenger-door animation
  • Ten-second loaders at scheduled stops
  • Queued ride confirmation clears at passenger boarding
Stay on the road

Your next fare can find you first.

Complete more than 90% of a ride and a new floating request may appear. Accept within five seconds to queue it behind the current passenger—or ignore it and wait 5–10 seconds for another opportunity.

Finish the current ride

The accepted request never overwrites the passenger already in your car.

Keep the next fare queued

The phone preserves the passenger, route, payout, and request type.

Drop off and continue

TaxiDriver automatically redirects you to the queued pickup.

Clear the confirmation

The accepted indicator disappears as soon as that passenger starts boarding.

Make it yours

A phone that fits your screen and driving style.

Open settings from the phone, change the experience, save, and return directly to the previous screen. Preferences are validated and stored outside the mod archive.
